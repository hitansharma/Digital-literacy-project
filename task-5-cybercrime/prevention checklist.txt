Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

1. Always verify payment requests before approving them on UPI apps.
2. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone.
3. Do not click on suspicious links received through emails or messages.
4. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication whenever possible.
5. Avoid making financial transactions on public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks.
6. Double-check the recipient’s name before sending money through UPI.
7. Install apps only from official app stores such as Google Play Store.
8. Regularly check your bank account and transaction history for unusual activity.

If you become a victim of cybercrime, report it immediately:

National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
Cybercrime Helpline: 1930
